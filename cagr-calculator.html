CAGR explanation

CAGR(Compound year growth rate) is an important indicator of Investment return, it shows the average year growth rate of Investment within a specific time period.

Calculate formula

CAGR = (Final Value / Initial Value)^(1/year number) - 1

Usage scenarios

  • Stock Investment return analysis
  • Fund performance evaluation
  • Enterprise income growth analysis
  • Real Estate Investment return Calculate
  • Project Investment return evaluation

Notes

  • CAGR assumes Investment return is evenly distributed
  • Does not consider intermediate fluctuations and risk factors
  • Applicable to long-term Investment analysis
  • Need to be evaluated comprehensively with other indicators
