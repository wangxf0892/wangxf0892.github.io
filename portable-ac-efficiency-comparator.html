Energy Efficiency Tips

🌡️ Optimal Temperature Settings

Set your AC to 78°F (26°C) for optimal efficiency. Each degree lower increases energy consumption by 6-8%.

🏠 Smart Insulation

Proper insulation can reduce cooling costs by 20-30%. Seal windows, doors, and use thermal curtains.

⏰ Programmable Thermostats

Use programmable thermostats to automatically adjust temperature when you're away or sleeping.

🌿 Natural Cooling

Use ceiling fans, open windows at night, and plant shade trees to reduce AC dependency.
